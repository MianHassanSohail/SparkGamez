Our Story

Founded in 2019 by a team of passionate developers and creative minds, Spark Games ignited from a shared vision to create games that spark joy, creativity, and lasting connections between players across the globe.

From our energetic beginnings as an indie studio to becoming a bright light in the gaming industry, we've consistently delivered innovative experiences that light up players' worlds across mobile, PC, VR, and web platforms.

Today, we continue to fuel the future of gaming with bold ideas, vibrant visuals, and gameplay that sparks imagination and brings communities together worldwide.
