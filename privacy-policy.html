Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 19th, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Spark Gamez Studio (“we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile applications, websites, and related services (collectively, the “Services”).

This Privacy Policy does not apply to:

By using our Services, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

  • Device Information: device model, operating system and version, country or region, advertising identifiers (e.g., Google Advertising ID), network and carrier information.

Information Collected With Permission

We may collect additional information (such as an email address) only when you explicitly authorize it through your device’s permission system.

How We Use Information

  • Operate and maintain the Services

  • Provide support and updates
  • Analyze trends and usage
  • Display advertisements (personalized or non-personalized)
  • Detect fraud, abuse, and technical issues
  • Comply with legal obligations

Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For users in the EEA, UK, or Switzerland, we process personal data under the following legal bases:

  • Consent
  • Contractual necessity
  • Legitimate interests
  • Legal obligations

Consent may be withdrawn at any time via your device settings.

Advertising & Analytics

We use third-party advertising and analytics SDKs. These partners may collect device identifiers and usage data for ad delivery, analytics, and performance measurement.

Ads may be personalized or non-personalized depending on user consent, age settings, and applicable regional laws.

Sharing of Information

  • With service providers and SDK partners
  • To comply with legal requirements
  • To protect users and company rights
  • During business transfers (merger or acquisition)
  • With your explicit consent

We do not sell personal data for money.

Children’s Privacy (COPPA & Families Policy)

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13 (or under 16 where required).

Parents may request data deletion by contacting us.
