Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1st, 2022

This privacy policy applies to all mobile applications published by Spark Gamez Studio across all app stores and platforms, including but not limited to the Google Play Store, Apple App Store, and Amazon Appstore. You agree to the terms described in this Privacy Policy by using any of our apps.

1. Who We Are

Spark Gamez Studio is a mobile game developer that creates and publishes casual and simulation games for mobile platforms. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a safe experience for all users of our games.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Device Information: Including device model, operating system, device identifiers (e.g., Android ID, IDFA, IDFV, Advertising ID), and language preferences.
  • Usage Data: Such as gameplay activity, in-game behavior, session duration, crash reports, and performance metrics.
  • Location Data: Approximate geographic location, inferred from IP address.
  • Ad Interaction Data: Data related to the ads you see and interact with in our apps.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data to:

  • Operate and improve our games
  • Deliver personalized and non-personalized ads
  • Analyze gameplay behavior and app performance
  • Detect bugs, crashes, or fraudulent behavior
  • Comply with legal requirements

4. Third-Party Services

We integrate with the following third-party platforms and services to enable advertising, monetization, and analytics in our apps:

IronSource

Ad mediation and monetization. It may collect device IDs, IP addresses, and engagement metrics.

Supplementary ad provider. Collects demographic and behavioral data.

These partners may use cookies, SDKs, or similar technologies for ad tracking and personalization.

5. Children's Privacy

Our apps are not intended for children under 13 (or under the age of digital consent in your region). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we discover such data has been collected, we will delete it promptly.

6. User Rights and Choices

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to:

  • Access and review your personal information

You can manage ad preferences by:

  • Adjusting privacy settings on your device

  • Uninstalling our apps at any time

We implement appropriate safeguards to protect your information. However, no internet-based service can guarantee absolute security.
